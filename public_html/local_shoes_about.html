All About ShoeCo

What We Are All About:

We are located in Indianola, Iowa about a 30 minutes drive from Des Moines. we carry a wide array of shoes for everyone, from crocks to running shoes. we have partneerships with all top selling brands such as Nike, Adidas, Puma, Vans, and Under Armour so all of our customers will be satifified with our selections.

For athletes we have many sport specific shoes, such as indoor shoes for playing futsal, basketball shoes, tennis shoes. Furthermore we carry many types of cleats specific for all sports such as soccer, football and baseball.

Whether you are a pro athlete or just like walking around your neighborhood, we have shoes for everyone! Big feet or small, all feet can find the perfect fit here at ShoeCo.

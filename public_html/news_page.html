Volunteering: September 6th, 2021

On Labor Day, our company had the day off and we decided as a company to help around the Indianola community. All the opportunities we had were endless:

  • Mowed Lawns
  • Cleaned Cars
  • Visit Local Restaurants
  • Go to the homeless shelter

We love volunteering within our community to those who have always supported our company from the beginning to now. This helps grow not only our business but our community as a whole. We will try to get the word out when we are volunteering and helping out the community so others can come join in on the fun. We want others to come out of their way to not only visit Indianola but because they love the people and of course the shoes!
